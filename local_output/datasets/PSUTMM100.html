PSU Taiji MultiModal (PSU-TMM100) Dataset

This research is funded in part by Penn State College of Engineering Dean's Office, NSF IIS-1218729, NSF IIS-1909315.

The PSU Taiji MultiModal (PSU-TMM100) Dataset contains 100 Taiji motion sequences, 24-form Simplified Taiji-Quan, aka Tai Chi, performed by 10 human subjects. The dataset includes time-synchronized measurements:      - motion capture markers and body joints recorded using Vicon Nexus 2.6.1 - foot pressure recorded using Tekscan F-scan 7.0 insole sensors - 1080p video from two views recorded using Vicon Nexus 2.6.1
