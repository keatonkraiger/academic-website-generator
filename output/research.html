Explicit and Implicit Regularity Perception

This research formulates a symmetry group theory-based regularity space and constructs a computational platform for pattern-seeking algorithms that are principle-driven and human perception-inspired.

Symmetry Group-based Regularity Perception in Human and Computer Vision

This research integrates theoretical, experimental, and algorithmic thrusts to construct a novel conceptual framework for predicting and understanding the full range of regularity perception, both in humans, by measuring human brain activation and behavior, and in machines, through a computational framework for adaptive symmetry detection in computer vision.
